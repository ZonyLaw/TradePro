About

This tool provides insights on the direction of trade within the next 4 hours. There are three version of the models trained on key parameters relating to:

  • MA20, MA50, MA100
  • Bollinger Band
  • market trend strength

Traders use technical indicators and fundamentals to confirm a direction, but these trades are based on experience and there are many different combinations. With machine model, it helps decipher these patterns and provide an indication of the direction. Why technical analysis works because it captures the behaviour of the commercial trader who already have execute their view while retail trader try to make sense of the direction. This tool will help provide some clarity and give you a sense what is going to unfold.
