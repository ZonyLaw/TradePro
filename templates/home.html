About

This tool provides insights on the direction of trade within the next 4 hours. There are three version of the models trained on key parameters relating to:

  • MA20, MA50, MA100
  • Bollinger Band
  • market trend strength

Often, we use technical indicators and fundamentals to confirm a direction, but these are based on experience and there is too many different combinations. With machine model, it helps decipher these patterns and gives an good indication of the direction. It is found commercial trading determine the major direction despite how we may view the news, commercial trader already have their view. This tool will help capture their behaviour and give confidence in the market direction.
