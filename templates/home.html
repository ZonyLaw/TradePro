About

This is a tool to help give you a sense of the direction at the hour. It is models trained on some key parameters like:

  • MA20, MA50, MA100
  • Bollinger Band
  • market trend strength

It will tell you whether the trend is mostly oscillating or trending in a particular direction. Often, we use technical indicators and fundamentals to confirm a direction, but these are based on experience and there is too many different combinations. With machine model, it helps decipher these patterns and gives an good indication of the direction. It is found the pattern proceed the news because commercial trading knows more than retail and already has set the trend. The big players have already placed their bet.
