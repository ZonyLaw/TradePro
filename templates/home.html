About

Do you find yourself lost and not knowing what market direction is doing?

This is a tool to help give you a sense of the direction at the hour. It is trained on some key parameters like:

  • MA20, MA50, MA100
  • Bollinger Band
  • market trend strength

It will tell you if the trend is mostly oscillating or trending in a direction. Often we have many confirmation indicator but how do we put weights on them. This machine model will help give you a better sense on this by examining historical data and make those correlation.
