About

Do you find yourself lost and not knowing what market direction is doing?

This is a tool to help give you a sense of the direction at the hour. It is trained on some key parameters like:

  • MA20, MA50, MA100
  • Bollinger Band
  • market trend strength

It will tell you whether the trend is mostly oscillating or trending in a particular direction. Often, we have many confirmation indicators, but how do we assign weights to them? This machine model will help provide a better sense of this by examining historical data and making those correlations.
